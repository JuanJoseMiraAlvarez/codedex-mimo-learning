José Saramago

José Saramago was a Portuguese writer and Nobel Prize winner.
He was born on November 16, 1922, in Azinhaga, Portugal, and died on June 18, 2010, in Lanzarote, Spain.
Saramago is known for his unique narrative style and philosophical themes.
